<h4>What is GRAIN?</h3>
GRAIN is a first of its kind, openly available, and globally consistent dataset of the world's agricultural irrigation canals. It is based on data sourced from OpenStreetMaps (OSM) and leverages a machine learning based workflow to fix the prevalent tagging issues in OSM. GRAIN covers 95 countries and currently maps pver 3.8 million km of irrigation canals.
